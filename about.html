About Niq Ammar Soffian

Niq Ammar Soffian is a modern and dynamic law firm committed to delivering practical, strategic, and client-focused legal solutions. Established with the vision of providing high-quality legal services grounded in professionalism, integrity, and dedication, the firm serves a diverse range of clients including individuals, businesses, and organisations across Malaysia.

The firm offers a wide spectrum of legal services, including civil litigation, corporate and commercial advisory, criminal defence, property and real estate matters, as well as family law. Our lawyers bring together experience from various areas of legal practice, allowing the firm to adopt a multidisciplinary approach when addressing complex legal challenges.
