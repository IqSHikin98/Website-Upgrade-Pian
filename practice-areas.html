Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal services across a broad range of practice areas.

Civil Litigation

The firm maintains a strong civil litigation practice representing individuals, businesses, and organisations in a wide range of disputes. Our lawyers regularly handle complex matters involving contractual disputes, shareholder conflicts, debt recovery, professional negligence, and commercial litigation. We understand that disputes can have significant financial and reputational consequences.

As such, we work closely with our clients to develop effective litigation strategies while also exploring alternative dispute resolution methods such as negotiation and mediation where appropriate. Our litigation team regularly appears before the Magistrates' Courts, Sessions Courts, and the High Court of Malaya, providing dedicated representation throughout every stage of the legal process.

Corporate & Commercial

Niq Ammar Soffian advises businesses and corporate clients on a wide range of corporate and commercial matters. Our services include company formation, corporate structuring, shareholder agreements, joint venture arrangements, and regulatory compliance.

We assist clients in drafting and reviewing commercial agreements to ensure that their legal interests are properly protected. Our lawyers also provide strategic advice on corporate governance, risk management, and business transactions. By combining legal knowledge with a practical understanding of the business environment, we aim to support our clients in making informed decisions that contribute to the growth and sustainability of their organisations.

Criminal Law

Our criminal law practice is dedicated to protecting the rights of individuals facing criminal investigations or prosecution. We provide defence representation in a broad spectrum of matters, including offences under the Penal Code, drug-related charges, white-collar crime, and other criminal proceedings.

We recognise the seriousness of criminal allegations and the impact they may have on a person's life, livelihood, and reputation. Our approach is focused on careful case preparation, strategic defence planning, and strong courtroom advocacy. We ensure that every client receives fair, confidential, and professional legal representation from the earliest stage of the proceedings.

Property & Real Estate

The firm provides legal services for both residential and commercial property matters. Our work includes sale and purchase agreements, tenancy arrangements, property financing, perfection of transfer and charge, and advice on title-related issues.

We understand that property transactions require precision and careful attention to detail. Our lawyers work closely with clients, financial institutions, developers, and other stakeholders to ensure that transactions are completed smoothly and efficiently. We also assist in resolving disputes involving property ownership, contractual obligations, and land-related matters.

Family Law

Family law matters often involve deeply personal and sensitive issues. At Niq Ammar Soffian, we approach these matters with compassion, discretion, and professionalism. Our services include advice and representation in divorce proceedings, child custody and access arrangements, maintenance claims, and other family-related disputes.

We aim to help clients navigate emotionally challenging circumstances with clear legal guidance and practical solutions. Where appropriate, we encourage amicable resolution through negotiation and settlement, while remaining prepared to protect our clients' interests through formal court proceedings when necessary.
